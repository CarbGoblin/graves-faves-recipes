Dutch Oven Bread

Beautiful flour-dusted crust exterior with a fluffy, soft inside

This is a super easy, beginner friendly bread thats great to try out on a cool fall day or to compliment any soup or stew!

With just a single hour to proof and only 4 ingredients, here is a bread recipe you won't need to plan days in advance!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 packet of activated dry yeast, 2 1/4 tsp
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups warm water
  • Thats it!

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a mixing bowl, stir with a fork until a rough dough forms.
  2. Knead with your hands on a lightly floured surface until a smooth dough forms, then return dough to a lightly oiled bowl.
  3. Cover the bowel with a slightly damp towel and place somewhere warm and free from drafts for 45 minutes to an hour, until dough doubles in size.
  4. During the last 10 minutes of proofing, place your dutch oven with the lid in the oven and pre-heat to 425F.
  5. Once dough has doubled in size, punch it down and knead a few more times.
  6. Very carefully remove your dutch oven from the hot oven and line with parchment paper, sprinking a little flour on the top and bottom of the dough then place in the dutch oven to cook!
  7. Replace the lid, then return the dutch oven to the oven to cook with the lid on for 35 minutes, then 10 more with the lid removed.
  8. Carefully take your dutch oven out, and remove your bread from the dutch oven. Let cool for 5-10 minutes and enjoy!
