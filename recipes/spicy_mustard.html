Spicy Mustard

This mustard is the perfect blend of garlicy and hot. I always keep a jar on hand! This recipe makes about 16oz.

Ingredients

  • 5-6 bulbs of garlic, peeled
  • 4-6 hot peppers, I like habanero, but Trinidad Scorpion, Carolina Reaper, and Ghost peppers all work great!
  • Enough oil to confit the garlic and peppers
  • 1 Tbsp black mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 Tbsp lime juice
  • 2 Tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Pinch of xanthan gum

Directions

  1. Peel the garlic and cut the stems off the peppers, confit with peppers, garlic, and salt in the oil at 210F for 1 hour.
  2. Powder the mustard seed and combine with chili powder, lime juice, and vinegar. Chill until confit is finished.
  3. Remove peppers and garlic from the oil and place into a blender. Add 1/4 cup of the oil, a pinch of xanthan gum, black pepper, and the mustard seed mixture. Blend lightly to avoid creating a mayo-like paste.
  4. If you prefer a thinner mustard, add water to preference.
