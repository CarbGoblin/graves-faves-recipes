Vegetable Lo Mein

Lo Mein noodles and veggies

This lo mein recipe is quick and easy to make and you'll swear its as good as your usual takeout spot!

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. Pack of Lo Mein Noodles*
  • 1 Tbsp Sesame oil
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 cups Crimini mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 Bell pepper, julienned
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cup Snow peas
  • 3 cups Baby spinach
For the Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp Soy sauce
  • 2 Tsp Sugar
  • 1 Tsp Sesame oil
  • 1/2 Tsp Ground ginger
  • 1/2 Tsp Sriracha, or more to taste

Directions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, ginger, and Sriracha; set aside.
  2. In a large pot of boiling water, cook noodles according to package instructions; drain well.
  3. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok over medium high heat. Add garlic, mushrooms, bell pepper, and carrot. Cook, stirring frequently, until tender, about 3-4 minutes. Stir in snow peas and spinach until the spinach has wilted, about 2-3 minutes.
  4. Stir in noodles and soy sauce mixture, and gently toss to combine.
  5. Serve and enjoy!

*can be replaced with udon or other suitable noodles
